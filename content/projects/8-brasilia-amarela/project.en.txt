Text: 

_Brasília Amarela_ (Yellow Brasilia) is a magazine designed as an academic project for a class at University of Brasilia. It's content has a comical approach about Brasilia's past, present and future.

Illustrations aside made by me.

This project is the result of a collaboration between [Felipe Tavira](http://felipetavira.com/), [Anais Almeida](https://www.behance.net/anaisflying) and myself. Find out some more [here](https://www.behance.net/gallery/32380577/Brasilia-Amarela).
